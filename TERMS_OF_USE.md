## 1. Description
BirdNET-STM32 provides resources for training and deploying tiny audio classification models for bioacoustics on embedded devices, specifically targeting the STM32N6 series. This project is part of the BirdNET ecosystem for biodiversity monitoring.

Development and support involve the K. Lisa Yang Center for Conservation Bioacoustics (Cornell University) and Chemnitz University of Technology. The Software is provided for research, evaluation, and conservation purposes.
